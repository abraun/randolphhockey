2025 Randolph Middle School Hockey Tryouts

Dear Players and Parents,

Thank you for signing up for the 2025 Randolph Middle School Hockey tryouts! Here's everything you need to know before hitting the ice:

Tryout Dates & Time

  • Sunday, May 18th at 7:00 PM
  • Monday, May 19th at 7:00 PM

Location: SportsCare Arena, Randolph

Tryout Fee

  • $100 (includes both tryout sessions + 2025 game jersey and socks)
  • Payment must be made prior to taking the ice
  • PayPal: @randolphrecicehockey
  • Venmo: @joef985
  • Or bring cash/check to check-in

Waiver Requirement

A waiver must be completed and submitted before taking the ice.

Waivers will be emailed to all registered participants prior to tryouts. Extra copies will be available at sign-in.

What to Wear for Tryouts

To help identify players by grade level:

6th Graders
White/Light Jersey
8th Graders
Blue/Dark Jersey
7th Graders
Any Jersey + Pinnie

Post-Tryout Registration

  • Players selected for a team will be required to register officially via Randolph Community Pass
  • Registration opens in mid-June
  • Registration is required prior to any August practices or games
  • Season fee will be $500

Game Jerseys

  • Jersey numbers will be assigned based on player survey responses and availability
  • Game jerseys and socks are included for players who attend tryouts
  • Players who do not attend tryouts but still plan to participate will need to purchase jerseys directly which may have a higher cost

Help Spread the Word

If you recognize any middle school players who plan to try out but aren't yet on the current TeamSnap list, please reach out to them and encourage them to register ASAP so they don't miss out!

Please feel free to reach out with any questions. We're looking forward to a great tryout and an exciting 2025 season of Rams Hockey!
